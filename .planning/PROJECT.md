# Che Construction PLC — Website

## What This Is

A professional website for Che Construction PLC, an Ethiopian construction company offering building construction, road & infrastructure, and renovation services. The site targets both government and private sector clients across Ethiopia, with a strong SEO strategy to dominate local search queries like "best construction company in Ethiopia."

## Core Value

Rank #1 for Ethiopian construction-related search queries while presenting Che Construction as a credible, professional, and established construction firm.
